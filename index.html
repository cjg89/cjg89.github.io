About Me

I love solving complex problems through design and code.

My decade+ of experience in web development has given me the opportunity to be involved in small-scale and large enterprise websites and applications. I’ve contributed everything from wireframes to designs, to front-end and back-end code, to performance and accessibility audits and cleanup, to end-user documentation and support…and truly feel that my understanding of the entire project lifecycle has made me a better developer.

While I still consider myself “full-stack”, front-end development and UX are what I specialize in, with particular emphasis on writing semantic and accessible code. I currently work full-time as a senior front-end developer.
